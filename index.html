PROCESS

How It Works

Simple three-step process with advanced security

Step 1: Generate QR

Instructor starts an attendance session and displays a unique, time-limited QR code on the classroom screen.

Step 2: Scan Code

Students scan the QR code using their mobile device camera within the attendance window.

Step 3: Verify GPS

System automatically captures GPS location and verifies the student is within 50m of the classroom.
